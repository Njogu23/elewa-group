Elewa NV, a multi-stakeholder coo(r)peration

Elewa NV is a multi-stakeholder cooperation based out of Brussels with subsidiaries in Belgium and East-africa. Elewa NV supports the operations of all brands associated to the Elewa family.

More than a company, elewa is a home for talents across the world. A place which allows for exploration and growth. As each finds their place, on the path unwinding.
